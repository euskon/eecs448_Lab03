Teddy's Biography

Biography:

The Teddy bear they call Ted is an individual who excels. Provided he is already stuffed. Every morning when Ted gets out of bed he is floppy and unstuffed, however, not for long shall this bear stay limp. Once fluffed he begins his day; This is also in accordance with how he came into existence. Day in and day out, since day one. Packed, Puffed, and Prepared is Ted's mantra for success. Always bring the necessary supplies for all sessions, all the time.
